The Tenants of Flat Nr. 5

The flat on the 5th floor was divided into two in the 1920s on the initiative of the pre-revolutionary tenant General Nikolaev, and three rooms on the side of the front staircase were bought by a certain Freiman from Mogilev.

Yakov Freiman

The fourth room in this half of the flat was bought in 1927 by his fellow countryman Veniamin Rafalovich. In 1929, without waiting for compaction, Freiman sold the big room to his cousin and business partner (co-owner of a brewery) during the NEP, Dovid-Irma Pevzner.

So, a large Jewish family from Mogilev settled in the hall on the 5th floor in the late twenties. The deeply religious Dovid-Irme and his wife Haya-Rohl and some children, who had departed from the observance of tradition, in one room.
